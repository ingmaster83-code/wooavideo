Frequently Asked Questions

My GIF is too large. How do I reduce the size?

Shorten the clip duration, lower the output width, or reduce the FPS to 8–10. A 5-second clip at 480px and 10 FPS typically produces 5–15 MB.

Why does conversion take so long?

GIF conversion includes a palette generation step to optimize colors, which takes longer than a simple format conversion. Try a shorter clip or lower resolution first.

Are my files uploaded to the internet?

No. All processing happens entirely within your browser — your files are never uploaded to any server. This zero-server architecture keeps your data 100% private, safe for sensitive documents and enterprise security environments.
