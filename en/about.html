Browser Processing Limitations

WooaVideo uses FFmpeg.wasm to process video entirely in your browser with no server involved. This guarantees complete privacy, but processing can be 10–20x slower than a native app.

  • Files over 200 MB may take several minutes to process
  • On first use, approximately 30 MB of engine files will be downloaded
  • Mobile devices may process slower than desktops
  • A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge) is recommended
