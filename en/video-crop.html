Frequently Asked Questions

Can I preview the crop area before processing?

A live preview is not currently available. After selecting a file, the expected output resolution is shown in the options panel when you choose a ratio.

Will the video quality change after cropping?

The video is re-encoded in H.264 after cropping. With default settings the quality loss is minimal, though slight differences from the original are possible due to compression.

Are my files uploaded to the internet?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Files are never sent to any server. Your content stays completely private.
