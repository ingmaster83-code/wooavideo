Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Which video formats are supported?
Audio can be extracted from most video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, WebM, MKV, FLV, and WMV.
Q. Will the audio quality be degraded?
Choosing WAV or FLAC gives lossless extraction. For MP3 or AAC, quality may slightly decrease if the bitrate is lower than the original. 192 kbps or higher is recommended.
Q. Are files uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser.
