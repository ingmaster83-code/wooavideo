Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is CRF?
CRF (Constant Rate Factor) controls video compression quality. Lower values give better quality with larger files; higher values give smaller files with lower quality. 18–23 is high quality, 24–28 is standard, 29–35 is small file size.
Q. How much will the file size be reduced?
It depends on the original codec and content, but at CRF 26, you can generally expect a 40–60% reduction. Files that are already heavily compressed may see less reduction.
Q. Are files uploaded to a server?
No. All compression happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any external server.
