Frequently Asked Questions

I want to keep the original voice and just add background music.

Select Mix mode. It blends the original audio track with your new audio file. Replace mode removes the original audio entirely.

Will the video quality change after processing?

No. Replace mode copies the video stream as-is, so there is no quality change. Mix mode also copies the video and only re-encodes the audio.

Are my files uploaded to the internet?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Files are never sent to any server. Your content stays completely private.
