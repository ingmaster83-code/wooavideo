Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

🔒
Core Principle: Files are never sent to a server
All video processing on WooaVideo takes place exclusively inside your browser. Files you load are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

WooaVideo collects only the following information:

  • Visit statistics: Anonymized visitor statistics (page views, session counts, device types, etc.) are collected via Google Analytics 4 (GA4). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Ad cookies: Cookies may be used by Google AdSense and Coupang Partners for ad-related services.

2. File & Video Data

All video, audio, and image files processed on WooaVideo are handled as follows:

  • Files are never transmitted to a server
  • All processing occurs only in your browser's memory (RAM)
  • Files are automatically cleared from browser memory after processing completes
  • We have no ability to access or store the contents of your files

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4: Visit statistics collection. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Ad serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • Coupang Partners: Affiliate marketing service. Coupang Privacy Policy
  • FFmpeg.wasm: Browser-based video processing engine (loaded from CDN). File data is never sent to the CDN.

4. Cookies

WooaVideo uses cookies for Google Analytics and advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, some service features may be limited as a result.

5. Policy Changes

If this Privacy Policy is updated, changes will be reflected on this page. Significant changes will be announced on the homepage.

This policy is effective as of January 1, 2026.
