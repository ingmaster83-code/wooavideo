Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add an image or logo watermark?

Currently only text watermarks are supported. Image watermark support is planned for a future update.

Can I use non-ASCII characters in the watermark?

The built-in FFmpeg font does not fully support non-Latin characters. For best results, use ASCII letters, numbers, and symbols like ©, ®, and @.

Are my files uploaded to the internet?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Files are never sent to any server. Your content stays completely private.
